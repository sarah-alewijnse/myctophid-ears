Procedure for Diet Data

Diet data average from literature according to suitable locations
Where muscle tissue is used, -1%o DIC to account for fractionation

Procedure for DIC

DIC and Souss effect from Tagliabue et al. 2008 PISCES-A
	Before 1990, Final DIC = DIC-(Souss*((1990-Year)/10))
	After 1990, Final DIC = DIC+(Souss*((1990-Year)/10))
Standard deviation is 0.202

Final M Values

Use means for overall value
